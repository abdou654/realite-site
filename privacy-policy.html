Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

Realite Bot ("we," "us," "our," or "the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Discord User Information

  • User IDs: We collect and store Discord user IDs to identify users and maintain user data.
  • Server IDs: We store Discord server (guild) IDs where the bot is used.
  • Username & Avatar: Your Discord username and avatar may be displayed when using bot features.
  • Game State Data: For Among Us game functionality, we may store game-related data temporarily.

Usage Data

  • Commands you use within the bot
  • Timestamps of your interactions
  • Server configurations and preferences
  • Message content only when directly necessary for bot functionality

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your password or authentication tokens
  • Your email address (unless voluntarily provided)
  • Personal information outside of Discord
  • Chat messages (unless explicitly required for a feature)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Bot Functionality: To provide and maintain bot services and features
  • User Identification: To identify you and maintain your user profile/statistics
  • Feature Enhancement: To improve and optimize the bot's performance
  • Game Management: To manage Among Us game states and roles
  • Moderation: To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage & Security

Data Storage: User data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, and deletion.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Retention

  • User IDs: Retained for the duration you use the bot and 90 days after last interaction
  • Game Data: Temporary storage only (deleted when game session ends)
  • Server Settings: Retained until the bot is removed from the server
  • Activity Logs: Retained for 30 days for security and moderation purposes

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. Your information is shared only with:

  • Discord: We interact with Discord's API to provide bot services (subject to Discord's Privacy Policy)
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that assist with hosting and database management
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request what data we store about you
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • Right to Correction: You can request updates to inaccurate information
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can disable the bot or request removal of specific features

To exercise these rights, contact us on Discord or via email.

7. GDPR & Data Protection Compliance

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, your personal data is processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the rights outlined in Section 6 above.

8. Children's Privacy

Realite Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date.

Continued Use: Your continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Discord: Message our support server
  • Email: privacy@realitebot.com (if applicable)
  • GitHub Issues: Submit a privacy concern
